Particular Research Interests:
1) Renewable-energy power system's stability, control, and optimization
2) Data-analytics for smart grid applications
3) Microgrid and multi-energy system
4) Electric vehicle to grid (V2G)

Current Projects
1) Big-Data Analytics for Future Smart Grid Security Assessment, Control and Optimization
2) Development of a Planning Tool for Electric Vehicle Impact Analysis and Urban Living Electrical Infrastructure Planning
3) Electro-Mechanical Interactions in Starter Generator
4) Grid Compliance for the Land based Power Generators
5) Grid-customer Integrated Resilience Assessment and Enhancement for Modern Power Systems IEMI Study on Power Grid
6) Research, development and demonstration of energy storage systems and technologies dedicated to renewable energy integration in remote locations
7) Site A - 2.4MWh/2.4MWh Lithium-ion Solutions: Work Packages 8 - 15 and 20
8) Stability-Constrained Robust Dispatch of Renewable Power Systems under Multiple Uncertainties
9) Technologies for Smart Power Plant
